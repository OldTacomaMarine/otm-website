The yacht Westward is powered by its original 1924 Atlas-Imperial geared engine, the oldest known Atlas-Imperial diesel that still runs. During a recent overhaul, Reilley had the crankshaft ground in place.

Engine Modifications

  • Cylinder lubricator removed.
