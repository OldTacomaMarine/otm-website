Atlas-Imperial model numbers are a combination of four different designations: cylinder number, block type, application, and cylinder volume. Block types were either "H" heavy-duty engines with individual cylinders and heads; or "E" en-block engines with a solid block and individual heads. Application could be "M" (marine), "S" (stationary), or "N" (industrial). Cylinder volume is the cubic volume in inches of a single cylinder. For example, a 4HM763 Atlas-Imperial is a marine heavy-duty diesel with four cylinders and a volume of 763 cubic inches per cylinder.
